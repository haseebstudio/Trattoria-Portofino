Our Story

A little corner
of Italy in Berlin

Tucked away on Gubener Straße in the lively Friedrichshain neighbourhood, Trattoria Portofino has been welcoming guests with the warmth and simplicity of authentic Italian cooking. Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries a little piece of Italy with it.

We make our pasta fresh every morning, bake our pizzas in a real stone oven, and source our fish from trusted suppliers — the same way it's been done in Italian trattorias for generations. Pair your meal with a glass from our handpicked Italian wine list and let the evening take its time.
